Our Story: From Mechanic to Trusted Towing Service Owner

Archers Towing Company was founded by a passionate mechanic with a dream to make Kenya's roads safer and less stressful for motorists. Daniel's journey began in Kariokor, Nairobi, where he worked tirelessly as a mechanic at a local garage.

One of the garage's regular customers was the owner of a flatbed tow truck. Impressed by Daniel's meticulous work and honesty, the customer offered him a job as a turnboy (driver's helper) and mechanic for his towing company.
